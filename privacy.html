I (Ashley Smith) am committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information when you use my website (www.ashleykaesmith.com).

This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information I collect, why I collect it, and how I use, store, and protect it, in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

Contact form: When you submit the contact form on this website, I also collect the following personal information:

Contact form: Personal information is collected directly from you when you submit the contact form. Form submissions are processed by Formspree, a third-party service based in the United States, in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

Website hosting: Data collected by GitHub Pages is used for the purpose of operating and maintaining the hosting infrastructure for this website.

Your information is not used for marketing.

Personal information is only disclosed to the third-party service providers described above (Formspree and GitHub Pages), to the extent necessary to operate this website.

Your information is not otherwise disclosed to third parties, and is only disclosed where required or authorised by law. Your personal information will not be sold.

Reasonable steps are taken to protect your personal information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access.

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry. Server data collected by GitHub Pages and Formspree is retained in accordance with their respective privacy statements (GitHub, Formspree).

This website does not set cookies or use analytics tools. Third-party services used for form processing (Formspree) may use transient cookies as part of their submission process. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

No information about your browsing behaviour is collected or monitored.
